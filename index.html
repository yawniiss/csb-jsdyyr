The community token zkGhost on the zkSync Network aims to bring together a community of people who may feel alone. The theme of zkGhost revolves around ghosts, which are often perceived as scary but also fascinating to many people.

zkGhost aims to be another option aside from centralized stablecoins for investors to store their wealth and gain exposure to Ethereum while awarding those who participate in the zkSync ecosystem. As always, never invest more than you are comfortable losing.
